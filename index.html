About
Hello! I am a second year M.S. & Ph.D. (Integrated) Student at the Language & Knowledge Lab at KAIST, advised by Minjoon Seo. My main research goal is to build large neural models that are applicable to real-world scenarios, by addressing limitations current neural models inherently have. Specifically, I am interested in (1) allowing neural models to be lifelong learners [Continual Knowledge Learning, TemporalWiki, Prompt Injection] (2) providing privacy and security guarantees for neural models [Knowledge Unlearning], and (3) enabling neural models to truly follow human instructions [Negated Prompts, Retrieval of Soft Prompts, Flipped Learning].
